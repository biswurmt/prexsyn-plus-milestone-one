\abstract{Synthesis-aware generative models like PrexSyn guarantee synthesizability but are constrained by their building-block libraries and reaction templates. We investigate whether post-generation modification methods (CReM, mmpdb, JT-VAE, LibINVENT) provide measurable value in expanding chemical coverage beyond repeated PrexSyn sampling. This work establishes the first systematic benchmark of modification methods as complements to synthesis-aware generation, with complementarity analysis to inform pipeline design for drug discovery.}

\keywords{molecular generation, synthetic chemistry, drug discovery, machine learning}
